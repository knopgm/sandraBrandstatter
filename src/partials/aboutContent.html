Hey! I’m Sandra Brandstätter — a freelance illustrator based in Berlin, Germany.

ABOUT

My work is both character and colour-driven, and whilst graphic, it retains a tactile warmth and quirkiness. I aim to show intimate moments in accessible worlds that have been featured in editorials, book covers, branding and advertising campaigns.

After graduating from the University of Plymouth, I briefly worked as a designer in Bristol before diving into the full-time world of illustration in 2017.
